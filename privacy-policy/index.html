Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share your personal data. All file processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Trendzlib ToolBox ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website and online tools.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use our tools, you may upload files or enter text. However:

  • All processing happens locally in your browser
  • We do not upload your files to our servers
  • We do not store any content you process
  • Your data never leaves your device

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, tools used
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand how our tools are used

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our online tools
  • Understand which tools are most popular
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance
  • Ensure our website works properly across different devices

4. Data Storage and Security

Your Files Are Safe: All file processing (PDF merging, image compression, etc.) happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We never see, access, or store your files.

For any technical data we collect:

  • We use industry-standard security measures
  • Data is anonymized wherever possible
  • We do not sell or share your information with third parties

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., remembering your preferences)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., for understanding site usage)
  • Hosting providers (e.g., Vercel for website hosting)
  • CDN providers (e.g., for fonts and libraries)

These services may collect limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Object to data processing

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Users

Our website is accessible globally. If you access our site from outside Nigeria, please note that any information collected may be transferred to and processed in Nigeria or other countries where our service providers operate.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@trendzlib.com.ng
  • Website: https://trendzlib.com.ng

Bottom Line: We respect your privacy. Your files are processed locally on your device and never uploaded to our servers. We collect minimal analytics data to improve our service, but we never sell or share your personal information.
